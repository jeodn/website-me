My Favourite Websites

Who doesn't like music?

Spotify is a popular music streaming service with hundreds of millions of monthly users and 82 million songs. I love Spotify because I love music. It’s the most convenient way for me to listen to my favourite artists.

How do you think I made this website?

One of my favourite resources for learning coding is W3Schools. It has full courses in clearly and concisely written articles for many languages; HTML included.. Personally, I prefer W3Schools over video tutorials as you can go at your own pace. You can skip the lengthy explanations and head straight into the content. However, since its explanations are so quick, I don’t recommend it for complete beginners as it’s easy to get lost.

The world at the tip of your fingers.

Never get lost again! Gone are the days of playing “Where’s Waldo?” on a huge physical map. Now, you can just search for where you’re going. It even tells you exactly how to get there! And you’re not constrained to just where you want to go; Google Maps and Street View let you explore the world, see different cultures, and visit different places - all at the click of a button.
