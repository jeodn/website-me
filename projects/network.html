My thoughts

I thought it would be really cool to see who I know and who they know. It's a really cool way to figure out where you spend your time and with who.

While we didn't explicitly cover graphs in class, we covered trees, so graphs weren't too much more complicated. The data was collected with a Python script that interacts with the LinkedIn website and scrapes data using BeautifulSoup. As for the visualization, I used pandas, NetworkX, and PyVis to generate this visualization.
