ABOUT JOSH

Who Is Josh?

Hey, my name is Josh Villas. As of March 2022, I am 15 years old, and am currently enrolled in St. Roch Catholic Secondary School. My family is made up of my parents, me and my sister.

How I Got To Canada

I was not born in Canada. I was born in the Metro Manila area of the Philippines, where I spent the first 7 years of my life. The first thing I noticed after I hopped off the plane in 2014 was that it’s really cold! A lot colder than the summer that I was used to. Since then, I have lived all of my life in Canada (except a couple trips to the US) and I am now a fully Canadian citizen!

My Hobbies

I love learning about new things. For example, I enjoy learning about computers and computer software, drawing, 3D modeling, linguistics, history, or whatever interests me at the moment. In particular, I like learning about new ways to make things.


See more of my projects on the GALLERY page.

My Plan For Life

My main career plan is to be a computer programmer. I think I would also be fine if I became a 3D artist or graphic designer; as long as it has something to do with computers, I’ll enjoy it.

Take It Easy

However, my greatest goal in life is to have fun. I will never prioritize my career over my personal life, mainly because I feel stress is life in the 21st century’s greatest enemy. I believe that, if I was extremely successful but overloaded with stress, I wouldn’t be successful after all.
